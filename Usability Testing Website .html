  • Discoverability: New and semi-skilled users struggled to locate search and category filters; clear, visible search bar is essential.
  • Clarity: Ambiguous labels (â€œSubscribeâ€ vs â€œFollowâ€) and dual search bars confused participants; explicit labeling and tooltips recommended.
